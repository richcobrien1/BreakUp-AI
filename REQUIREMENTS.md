# Breakup-AI: Platform Requirements & Roadmap

## Core Platform Requirements

### 1. Legal Definitions Database
**Objective:** Comprehensive state-by-state legal framework

#### Requirements:
- **All 50 States + DC Legal Definitions:**
  - Marital property definitions
  - Community property vs equitable distribution states
  - Separate property classifications
  - Commingling rules
  - Transmutation of assets
  - Spousal support/alimony types and qualifications
  - Child support calculations and guidelines
  - Asset valuation methodologies
  - Debt allocation rules
  - Retirement account division (QDRO requirements)
  - Business valuation in divorce
  - Professional degree/license valuation
  - Cohabitation laws (palimony, common-law)

- **Federal Legal Framework:**
  - Constitutional amendments relevant to marital rights
  - Federal tax implications (IRC Section 1041)
  - ERISA and pension protections
  - Social Security spousal benefits
  - Military divorce laws (USFSPA)
  - Federal bankruptcy impact on divorce
  - Immigration implications (VAWA protections)

#### Deliverables:
- Searchable legal definitions database
- State-by-state comparison matrices
- Plain-English explanations for each legal term
- Citation and source documentation
- Last-updated timestamps for accuracy

### 2. Multi-State Decision Trees
**Objective:** Navigate complex jurisdictional scenarios

#### Requirements:
- **Jurisdiction Determination:**

  - Multi-state asset location

  - Waiting periods by state
  - Statute of limitations concerns
  - Temporary orders timelines

  - Appeal windows

- **Cross-Border Considerations:**
  - International marriage/divorce recognition

#### Deliverables:
- Interactive decision tree tool
- "Which state should I file in?" calculator
- Timeline projections by jurisdiction
- Comparative analysis tools

  - Financial documentation list
  - Asset documentation list
  - Income documentation list
  - Debt documentation list

## Ethical Framework

### Unauthorized Practice of Law (UPL) Compliance:
- Clear disclaimers on all content
- Educational vs legal advice boundaries
- Attorney referral (not replacement)
- No document preparation service
- No case-specific legal advice

### User Protection:
- Privacy and confidentiality
- Safety-first design
- Trauma-informed content
- Crisis intervention protocols
- Mandatory attorney consultation warnings

### Accuracy Standards:
- Legal review by licensed attorneys
- State bar compliance verification
- Regular content audits
- Source citation requirements
- Correction/update protocols
